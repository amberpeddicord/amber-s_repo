About Amber:

Amber Peddicord is a junior at The University of Pittsburgh-Greensburg. She is a double major in English Literature and Communication and is on track to receive a Digital Studies Certificate. In addition to her studies, Amber is the secretary of the Alpha Alpha Delta Chapter of Sigma Tau Delta, the International English Honor Society. She is also a Green Scholar for Dr. Elisa Beshero-Bondar on the Digital Mitford Project. To learn more about this project, and Amber's work on it, visit the Digital Projects tab.
